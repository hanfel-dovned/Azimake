Welcome to Micro!

Micro is most fun on mobile. You can open it on your phone and add it to your homescreen.

Click the big circle at the bottom to explore apps.

Click the hammer on the left to build one of your own.

There's currently a bug with hosting providers preventing login buttons from working within Micro. Click the arrow in the bottom right and log in there instead.
